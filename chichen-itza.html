4) Chichén Itzá, Mexico

History:

Chichen Itza was a major Mayan city between the 7th and 10th centuries. Its most famous structure, El Castillo, is a pyramid that was used for astronomical and religious purposes..

Location:

Yucatán Peninsula, Mexico

Fun Fact:

The pyramid is designed so that during the equinox, a shadow of a serpent seems to slither down its steps.
